Gets the complete list of indices of final-state daughters of an MC particle.

Parameters
particlesMCtable with MC particles
indexindex of the MC particle
listvector where the indices of final-state daughters will be added
arrPDGFinalarray of PDG codes of particles to be considered final
depthMaxmaximum decay tree level; Daughters at this level (or beyond) will be considered final. If -1, all levels are considered.
stagedecay tree level; If different from 0, the particle itself will be added in the list in case it has no daughters.
Note
Final state is defined as particles from arrPDGFinal plus final daughters of any other decay branch.
Antiparticles of particles in arrPDGFinal are accepted as well.
